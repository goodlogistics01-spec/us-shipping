Frequently Asked Questions

Direct answers for importers comparing China to USA freight options, timing and delivery responsibility.

What is the best shipping method from China to the USA?

It depends on cargo size, urgency, budget and delivery address. Air freight is better for urgent cargo, sea freight for bulk shipments, and DDP can simplify door-to-door delivery.

Can you pick up cargo from multiple suppliers in China?

Yes. We can collect goods from different suppliers, consolidate them in China and arrange one shipment to the United States.

Do you support Amazon FBA shipping?

Yes. We support China to Amazon FBA warehouse shipping, including freight planning, carton handling, labeling support and delivery appointment coordination.

Can you handle oversized cargo?

Yes. We provide customized shipping plans for oversized, heavy or difficult cargo, including machinery, furniture and professional equipment.
