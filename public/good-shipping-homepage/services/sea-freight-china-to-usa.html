Frequently asked questions

Should I choose FCL or LCL sea freight?

FCL is usually better for larger shipments or cargo needing container control. LCL is suitable for smaller shipments that can share container space.

Can sea freight include final delivery?

Yes. Good Logistics can arrange ocean freight plus customs coordination and truck delivery to a warehouse, business, Amazon FBA or project site.

What affects the sea freight price?

Origin city, port pair, cargo volume, weight, commodity, delivery ZIP code, liftgate needs, oversized handling and customs scope all affect the quote.
