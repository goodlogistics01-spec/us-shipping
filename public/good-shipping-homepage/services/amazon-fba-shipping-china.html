Amazon FBA Shipping from China

China to Amazon FBA freight forwarding for sellers who need supplier pickup, carton handling, labeling support and appointment delivery.

Get a Quote

When this service fits

  • Amazon sellers shipping inventory from Chinese suppliers
  • Shipments requiring carton marks and delivery appointments
  • LCL, FCL, air freight or DDP routes to U.S. FBA warehouses
  • Sellers consolidating cargo from multiple Chinese suppliers

Service process

1. China Pickup

We coordinate supplier pickup, cargo details, package checks and export preparation before the shipment leaves China.

2. International Transit

We recommend the freight method based on cargo size, urgency, budget and destination requirements, then coordinate booking and status follow-up.

3. US Delivery

We coordinate customs-related handoff and final delivery to the destination address, including business, warehouse, FBA or project-site delivery when applicable.

Frequently asked questions

Can you deliver directly to Amazon FBA?

Yes. Good Logistics can coordinate delivery to Amazon FBA warehouses when shipment plans, labels and appointment requirements are provided.

Can you consolidate inventory from multiple suppliers?

Yes. Cargo can be collected from different suppliers, checked, consolidated and prepared before international shipping.

What FBA details are required?

Provide shipment IDs, carton labels, destination warehouse, carton count, dimensions, weight and any Amazon receiving instructions.

Ready to discuss your shipment?

Tell us your cargo type, supplier city, destination and timeline. Good Logistics Co., Ltd. will recommend a practical shipping plan.
