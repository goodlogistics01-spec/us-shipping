When this service fits

  • Furniture, massage chairs, machinery and industrial equipment

Service process

1. China Pickup

We coordinate supplier pickup, cargo details, package checks and export preparation before the shipment leaves China.

2. International Transit

We recommend the freight method based on cargo size, urgency, budget and destination requirements, then coordinate booking and status follow-up.

3. US Delivery

We coordinate customs-related handoff and final delivery to the destination address, including business, warehouse, FBA or project-site delivery when applicable.

Frequently asked questions

What counts as oversized cargo?

Cargo may be oversized when dimensions, weight, packaging or delivery access require special handling beyond standard parcel or pallet freight.

Can you handle oversized cargo to U.S. islands?

Yes. Hawaii, Alaska and Puerto Rico are core specialty destinations for Good Logistics, including difficult and oversized cargo.

What information is needed before booking?

Send product photos, packed dimensions, gross weight, supplier city, loading method and final delivery address details.

Ready to discuss your shipment?

Tell us your cargo type, supplier city, destination and timeline. Good Logistics Co., Ltd. will recommend a practical shipping plan.
