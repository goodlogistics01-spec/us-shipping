Service process

1. China Pickup

We coordinate supplier pickup, cargo details, package checks and export preparation before the shipment leaves China.

2. International Transit

We recommend the freight method based on cargo size, urgency, budget and destination requirements, then coordinate booking and status follow-up.

3. US Delivery

We coordinate customs-related handoff and final delivery to the destination address, including business, warehouse, FBA or project-site delivery when applicable.

Frequently asked questions

What should a DDP quote include?

A clear DDP quote should define origin pickup, export handling, international freight, customs clearance responsibility, duties/taxes scope and final delivery address.

Is DDP always the cheapest option?

Not always. DDP can simplify coordination, but the included scope must be compared carefully against standard freight options.

Can DDP be used for Amazon FBA?

Yes, when carton, labeling, appointment and delivery requirements are confirmed before booking.
