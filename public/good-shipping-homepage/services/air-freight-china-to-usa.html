Air Freight from China to USA

Fast air freight from Chinese suppliers to U.S. airports, Amazon facilities and commercial addresses with coordinated pickup and final delivery.

When this service fits

  • Urgent production replenishment or launch inventory
  • High-value goods that need shorter transit time
  • Samples, replacement parts and smaller commercial shipments
  • Cargo that needs predictable airport-to-door handling

Service process

1. China Pickup

We coordinate supplier pickup, cargo details, package checks and export preparation before the shipment leaves China.

2. International Transit

We recommend the freight method based on cargo size, urgency, budget and destination requirements, then coordinate booking and status follow-up.

3. US Delivery

We coordinate customs-related handoff and final delivery to the destination address, including business, warehouse, FBA or project-site delivery when applicable.

Frequently asked questions

How long does air freight from China to the USA take?

Typical air freight transit can be several days after cargo is ready, depending on pickup city, airline space, customs and final delivery address.

Can air freight include door delivery?

Yes. Good Logistics can coordinate China pickup, export handling, international air freight, customs coordination and U.S. final delivery.

What details are needed for an air freight quote?

Send cargo name, supplier city, destination address, package dimensions, gross weight, cargo value and target delivery date.

Ready to discuss your shipment?

Tell us your cargo type, supplier city, destination and timeline. Good Logistics Co., Ltd. will recommend a practical shipping plan.
