Frequently asked questions

Does Puerto Rico use U.S. customs rules?

Yes. Puerto Rico uses U.S. federal customs through CBP, so importers should prepare commercial invoice, packing list and commodity details like other U.S. shipments.

Can you ship door-to-door from China to San Juan?

Yes. Good Logistics can coordinate China pickup, international freight, customs-related handoff and final delivery to San Juan or other island destinations.

Is Puerto Rico suitable for Amazon sellers?

Yes. Amazon.com.pr sellers and other e-commerce sellers can use Puerto Rico freight planning when labels, delivery instructions and shipment details are confirmed.
