Frequently asked questions

How does shipping from China to Alaska work?

Cargo is usually collected from suppliers in China, moved by ocean or air freight to a U.S. gateway, then coordinated onward to Anchorage or the agreed Alaska destination.

Can you deliver to remote Alaska destinations?

Yes, standard Alaska destinations can be reviewed for door delivery planning. More remote addresses should be confirmed before quoting.

Can you handle oversized cargo to Alaska?

Yes. We review dimensions, weight, loading method, packaging and destination access before building an Alaska oversized cargo plan.
